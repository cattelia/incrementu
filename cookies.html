Incrementu's Cookie Policy

You may be wondering, what are cookies?

A cookie is a small text file that a website places on your computer or mobile device when you visit the site. It helps the site remember your actions and preferences (such as login, language, font size, and other display preferences) over a period of time, so you don't have to re-enter them whenever you come back to the site or browse from one page to another.

What does Incrementu do with these cookies?

Right now, nothing. But if we did!

User Experience
